Slow Cooker Tikka Masala

Ingredients

  1. 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breast,diced
  2. 1 medium diced white onion
  3. 3/4 cup chicken broth
  4. Juice of 1/2 lemon
  5. 1 tbsp ghee
  6. 1 tbsp minced ginger
  7. 2 tbps minced garlic
  8. 3 tsp garam masala
  9. 2 tsp turmeric
  10. 1.5 tsp salt
  11. 1 tsp cumin
  12. 1 tsp coriander
  13. 1 tsp chili powder
  14. 1 tsp paprika
  15. 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  16. 1/2 tsp black pepper
  17. 6 oz tomato paste
  18. 14oz can unsweetened, full fat, coconut milk
  19. 2 tsp arrowroot flour dissolved with watch to thicken, if desired
  20. cilantro to garnish, if desired
